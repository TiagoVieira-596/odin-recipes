Chicken Strogonoff

Description

The combination of juicy chicken meat and creamy sauce leaves anyone salivating just thinking about it. Originally created in Russia, the Brazilian version has some very particular differences.

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts cut into cubes
  • 1 clove of minced garlic
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper to taste
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 spoon of butter
  • 1/2 cup of ketchup
  • 1/3 cup of mustard
  • 1 cup of mushrooms
  • 1 glass of cream
  • shoestring potatoes to taste

Steps

  1. In a pan, mix the chicken, garlic, mayonnaise, salt and pepper.

  2. In a large frying pan, melt the butter and brown the onion.

  3. Add the seasoned chicken until it is golden.

  4. Add the mushrooms, ketchup and mustard.

  5. Add the cream and remove from the heat before boiling.

  6. Serve with white rice and straw potatoes.
